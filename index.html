Mizoram Labour Link

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13 May 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of personal information by the Mizoram Labour Link mobile application ("App"), developed and operated by the Labour, Employment, Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department (LESDE), Government of Mizoram, in collaboration with the ICT Department.

By downloading, registering on, or using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Registration details such as full name, phone number, email address (if provided), password, Aadhaar number (optional or as required), date of birth, gender, address, and profile photo.
  • Worker profile information including skills, work experience, preferred job categories, availability, education, and location (with consent).
  • Employer profile information including business/individual name, GST details, address, contact details, and nature of business.
  • Job-related information such as job postings, applications, messages, and chat communications between workers and employers.
  • Verification documents uploaded for identity verification and authentication purposes.

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information including model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage analytics, IP address, application version, and crash reports.
  • Location information (GPS) when enabled by the user for job matching and location-based services.
  • Cookies or similar technologies used for analytics and application functionality.

2. Purpose of Collection and Use of Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing user accounts.
  • Facilitating job matching between workers and employers.
  • Sending job alerts, notifications, SMS messages, and important service updates.
  • Enabling communication between users.
  • Verifying identities and preventing fraud, misuse, or unauthorized activities.
  • Improving application performance, analytics, and user experience.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Any additional purpose with user consent.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With other registered users where necessary for employment-related interactions.
  • With government authorities or law enforcement agencies when required by law.
  • With authorized service providers operating under confidentiality obligations.
  • In connection with government directives, mergers, acquisitions, or organizational restructuring.

All data sharing is conducted in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and other applicable laws of India.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect personal information.

  • User authentication and JWT-based authorization.
  • Encryption of sensitive information during transmission and storage.
  • Periodic security reviews and audits.

While we strive to protect personal information, no electronic system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Users may request deletion of their accounts and associated data, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.

6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable Indian laws, users have the right to:

  • Access, correct, and update their information.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Request deletion of personal data.
  • Lodge grievances regarding data processing.

Requests may be submitted through the contact details provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended only for individuals aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is identified, it will be removed promptly.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third-party services.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be communicated through the App or official Government channels. Continued use of the App after such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
